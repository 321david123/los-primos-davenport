Who We Are

Los Primos
means the cousins.

Two cousins — one a chef in a toque, one in his black apron — stand right in our logo, where they belong. Los Primos is a family-run Davenport taqueria built on authentic soft corn street tacos and one of the widest meat selections in the Quad Cities: al pastor, carnitas, barbacoa, lengua, tripa, pork belly and more.

Everything is fresh, hot and fast — from our house-made horchata (never from a machine) to MiMi’s tres leches cakes. We’ve recently settled into a new home on E Rusholme St, but nothing about the food has changed. Run, don’t walk.
